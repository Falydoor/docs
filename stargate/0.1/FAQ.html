Frequently asked questions

What should I do if I find a bug?

Feel free to post a repo on the Discord channel or if you are feeling ambitious file an issue with steps for reproduction and we’ll get to it as soon as possible. Please remember to include a full stack trace (if any) and the versions of Stargate and Cassandra that you are using.
